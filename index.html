small data Journalism was created to accomodate the Fall 2013 Data Journalism course, which is part of the Digital Certificate for Journalism at New York University (Continuing Studies).

I wanted my students to have a convenient place to find course-related information, files, and guides. Since the course aims to take a practical and generalized approach to data journalism, there's no reason not to open it to the world. And since it's on Github, feel free to submit fixes, suggestions, and criticism.

You can read more about the authorship and technical details behind this book in the About section. Otherwise, see the Table of Contents.
