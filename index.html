This is the online reference for the Data Journalism course in New York University's Certificate in Digital Journalism program.

This online reference was created to accomodate my upcoming class, Data Journalism (--TK), which is part of the Digital Certificate for Journalism at New York University (Continuing Studies). The class is only five weeks long but I wanted my students to get a full grasp of the scope and possible paths. Having it online, thorugh Github, makes it easy for me to edit and add, and is something that hopefully can be used by others not in the class.

You can read more about the authorship and technical details behind this book in the [About section](/about). Otherwise, keep scrolling down to see a Table of Contents.
